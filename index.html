About

I'm an engineering undergraduate with a focus on digital hardware design. My work spans FPGA development using Xilinx Ultrascale+ platforms, custom PCB design in Altium Designer, and embedded systems programming.

Currently, I'm building a multi-channel strain gauge data logger system, combining precision analog design with high-speed digital interfaces. I enjoy the challenge of bridging the gap between the analog and digital worlds.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy playing soccer, hiking, and exploring new places.
