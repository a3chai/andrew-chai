The Swiss Alps

Cherry Blossom Season

April 2024 3 weeks

Chasing cherry blossoms from Tokyo to Kyoto. The timing was perfect - full bloom in Ueno Park, serene temples in Nara, and the unforgettable Philosopher's Path lined with pink petals.
