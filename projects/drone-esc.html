Overview

The Drone ESC is a high-performance electronic speed controller designed for brushless DC motors used in drone and UAV applications. It features advanced FOC (Field Oriented Control) algorithms for smooth and efficient motor control.

The design prioritizes power efficiency, thermal management, and fast response times to meet the demanding requirements of multirotor flight controllers.

Key Features

  • Field Oriented Control (FOC) for smooth operation
  • High current capability with efficient MOSFETs
  • Integrated current sensing for protection
  • DShot and PWM signal compatibility
  • Active braking support
  • Thermal protection with temperature monitoring
  • Compact form factor for drone integration
  • STM32G4 microcontroller for fast control loops

Technical Specifications

Voltage Range 3S-6S LiPo (11.1V - 25.2V)

Design Details

Power Stage

The power stage uses low RDS(on) MOSFETs in a half-bridge configuration for each motor phase. Gate drivers with bootstrap circuits ensure fast switching times while minimizing switching losses.

Current Sensing

Phase currents are measured using low-side shunt resistors with dedicated current sense amplifiers. This enables precise FOC control and overcurrent protection.

Thermal Management

The PCB uses thick copper layers and thermal vias to conduct heat away from the MOSFETs. An onboard temperature sensor enables thermal throttling to prevent damage under extreme conditions.
